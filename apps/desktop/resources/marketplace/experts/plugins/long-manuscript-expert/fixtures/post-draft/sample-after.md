The center of gravity in AI+HR is shifting from "can it generate a paragraph"
to "can it carry a full delivery chain." What enterprise buyers want is not a
single copy block, but a workflow that can support recruiting, training, and
organizational analysis across multiple review steps.

Two outcomes matter most. First, whether research and planning can be turned
into reusable material. Second, whether the draft can move smoothly into review,
layout, and export. Providers that connect those steps are the ones most likely
to earn durable cooperation.
