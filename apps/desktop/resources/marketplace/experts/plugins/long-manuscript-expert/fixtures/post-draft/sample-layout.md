## Industry Overview

AI+HR has moved from isolated tools into process transformation. Enterprises no
longer buy a model that can only "write a paragraph"; they expect support for a
long chain of work including recruiting, training, performance, and
organizational analysis.

## Business Model Evolution

Since 2024, buyers have cared more about process fit, reviewable output, and
reusable knowledge than about model novelty alone. Competition therefore shifts
from raw generation capability to toolchain quality, knowledge structure, and
delivery governance.

## Three-Year Outlook

The next differentiator will not be speed alone. It will be whether multi-turn
collaboration, quality review, layout and export, and cross-session recovery
can form a stable workflow.
